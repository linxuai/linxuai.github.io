Company Introduction

Lu Jian Wan Xiang Information Technology (Shanghai) Co., Ltd. was established in June 2025 and is headquartered in Shanghai. The company specializes in information technology services and the application of artificial intelligence technologies. It was co-founded by two experienced engineers, both shareholders and core team members, who have many years of product development experience in well-known enterprises. One serves as the chief technology officer, independently leading the entire process from architecture design to full code development with a unique female perspective and strong innovation capabilities. The other has valuable experience in successfully developing a fashion app from scratch, with deep expertise in product implementation and user needs discovery. We are committed to the integrated innovation of technology R&D, product design, and related services, actively promoting the exploration and implementation of intelligent technologies in diverse scenarios.

The company adheres to the philosophy of "steady development, compliant operation, and continuous innovation", operates in accordance with laws and regulations, and is dedicated to creating long-term value for society and promoting the integration of technology and culture.
